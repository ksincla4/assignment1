Five Great Home Cooked Meals

Here is a ranking of my favorite home cooked meals. Click the links for recipies!

  1. Baked Macaroni and Cheese: my favorite meal of all time. My mom's is the greatest!
  2. Chicken Pot Pie: another home made staple. Perfect meal for the cold weather.
  3. Fried Chicken: I may be biased, but there is nothing like my mother's fried chicken. Want a bit of a kick? Fry it with mustard.
  4. Curry Shrimp: A west Indian powerhouse meal. 10/10 would recommend.
  5. Bourbon Chicken: have a bit of Louisiana in your own home with this tasty dish.
