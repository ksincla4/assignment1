Dining Out

Local Restaurants!

Here are some of my favorite restaurants in the New York City!

  • Woodland Brooklyn: a modern restaurant with West Indian Cuisine in Brooklyn's Park Slope neighborhood. If you want a place with cheap drinks, good food, and a live DJ for a crazy fun brunch, Woodland should be your next foodie destination!
  • Hill Country Barbeque: If you're looking for a place to enjoy genuine bbq food and live music, Hill Country BBQ is the place to go. It offers a variety of slow-cooked meats, an endless amount of flavorful sides, and great desserts to finish off the meal.

Vacation Restaurants!

Here are some of my favorite restaurants to go when we're on vacation!

  • Bahama Breeze: a taste of the island in the United States. If you wanna eat like you're in the Caribbean without spending the big bucks to ge there, you might want to check out Bahama Breeze! The closest one to Rochester is in a suburb of Cleveland, OH.
  • Mr. Chow's Smokehouse: If you're REALLY looking for authentic barbeque with a West Indian twist, Mr. Chow's Smokehouse in Trinidad and Tobago has something for you. Check it out if you ever find yourself in Trinidad's Woodbrook neighborhood.
